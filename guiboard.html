GUIBOARD


The Guiboard is a MIDI controller/instrument designed to mimic the layout of notes on a fretboard in a mechanical keyboard format. It can be configured in isomorphic (EADGC), guitar (EADGB), violin (CDGAE), or banjo with a full length fifth string (DDGBD) tunings. The rightmost keys on the top two strings operate as octave switches or alternatively semitone switches, transposing the keyboard up or down.
